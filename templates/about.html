About OTU Found

Our Mission

OTU Found is a secure, privacy-first lost and found system designed specifically for Ostime Teknik University. We solve the critical problems of traditional campus lost-and-found systems by using technology to verify ownership and protect user privacy.

Key Features

  • Secure Registration: Only @ostimteknik.edu.tr email addresses can register, keeping outsiders out.
  • Asymmetric Reporting: Detailed forms for lost items, but hidden details for found items to prevent false claims.
  • Verified Claims: No public phone numbers. Claims are reviewed by item owners before contact is shared.
  • Proof of Ownership: Claimants must provide specific details proving they own the item.
  • Data Integrity: Items are never permanently deleted—soft delete maintains records for university records.

How Claims Work

  1. User posts a lost or found item with photos and description.
  2. Other users browse and search for items.
  3. If a user believes they found/lost the item, they submit a claim with proof of ownership.
  4. The item's original poster reviews the claim and approves or rejects it.
  5. Upon approval, both parties are connected securely through the system.

Privacy & Security

We take privacy seriously. Phone numbers are never publicly displayed. Claims must be approved before any contact information is exchanged. The system uses secure email verification and authentication to ensure only authorized university members can participate.

Admin & Moderation

University security staff have access to an admin dashboard to manage categories, review disputes, and moderate items when necessary.
