How It Works

Report an Item

Post a lost or found item with photos and details. For found items, critical details are hidden from public view.

Browse all active items by type and category. Filter to find your lost item quickly.

💬 Submit a Claim

Claim an item by providing proof of ownership. Describe specific details that prove it's yours.

✔️ Verification

Item owners review your proof and approve or reject your claim. Communication happens securely through the system.
